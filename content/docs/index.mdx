The AI-Powered SaaS Startup Boilerplate is a comprehensive and fully-featured boilerplate for building and launching your AI-powered SaaS startup, leveraging the power of Next.js, Prisma, Neon, Auth.js v5, Resend, React Email, Shadcn/ui, and Stripe.

With this boilerplate, you gain a solid foundation for your journey to success. It streamlines the development process, provides a robust architecture, and offers a wide range of features out-of-the-box.

This documentation is your go-to resource for configuring and using the boilerplate effectively. It will guide you through the setup process, explain key concepts, and provide insights into how to leverage the various integrations and features.

Explore the documentation to learn more about the boilerplate's capabilities, customization options, and how to use it to build your AI-powered SaaS startup.

Ready to get started? Let's dive in and unlock the power of the AI-Powered SaaS Startup Boilerplate!
